The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Young Frankenstein

A comedy classic poking fun at classic horror.

Star Trek (2009)

A reboot done so well that the series could be rewritten
and still hold the attention of old and new fans alike.

Priscilla Queen of the Desert

Regardless of where you fall in the LGBTQIA alphabet soup,
this comedy will make you laugh and cry and laugh some more.
